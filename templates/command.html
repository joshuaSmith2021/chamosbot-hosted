!stats

>

!stats <minigame> <username(s)> --flags

  • Parameters
  • Flags
  • Examples

Parameters

The !stats command has two parameters: minigame, and username(s). Minigame is the minigame (ie skywars or bedwars) that the bot should get stats from. Username(s) is a space-separated list of usernames that the bot should fetch stats from.

Flags

With some minigames, you can specify a specific game mode to pull stats from. The format for one of these flags is --<number of teams>_<players per team> . For example, the flag for Bedwars solo would be --eight_one , because there are eight teams in the game, with each team having one player.
